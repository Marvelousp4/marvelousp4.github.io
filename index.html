Koopman
Linhao Bai, Hanyao Guo, Yunhai Han,
arXiv, 2025
arXiv

The Koopman operator systematically linearizes the nonlinear dynamics of robotic hands and manipulated objects, enabling policies to be derived analytically through pseudoinverse optimization rather than black-box reinforcement learning. Building on this theoretical foundation, a hybrid architecture integrates MLPs into Koopman observables to refine nonlinear dynamics modeling while preserving real-time control efficiency, allowing millisecond-level policy execution on low-cost Leaphand hardware.
